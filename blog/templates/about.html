About Us

Intellect Africa Group is a holding company based in Johannesburg, operating across all nine (9) provinces in South Africa, Southern Africa and beyond. It is within our philosophy that we pursue opportunities in the local and regional markets, both independently and in association with our strategic partners. Our focus is to provide home improvements, facilities management, road construction and IT services to a broad range of needs, sectors and budgets. We consistently apply innovative methods to ensure that we provide first class services to our clients whether on small or big projects. At Intellect Africa Group we strongly understand that infrastructure and information technology play a critical role to the economy, society and environment. It is our commitment to ensure that we integrate the three in our business operations.

Vision

To provide integrated services to our clients through the attraction of different professionals, technicians and graduates.



Mission

To provide our clients with the highest level of quality services at fair and competitive prices, and ultimately guaranteeing our clients top of the range services.
